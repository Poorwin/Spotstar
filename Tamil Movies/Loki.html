Lokesh Kangaraj

Lokesh Kanagaraj is a prominent Indian filmmaker and director known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born on March 14, 1986, he rose to fame with his debut film "Maanagaram" (2017), which received critical acclaim for its engaging narrative and realistic portrayal of urban life in Chennai.His next project, "Master" (2021), starring Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi, became a massive commercial success, showcasing his knack for creating entertaining yet thought-provoking narratives. Kanagaraj's films often explore themes of morality, justice, and the complexities of human relationships, resonating with audiences and critics.


1) Kaithi

  • About:

    • "Kaithi," released in 2019, is a Tamil action thriller directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring Karthi in the lead role. The film follows the story of Dilli, an ex-convict who is released from prison after a long sentence and is eager to reunite with his daughter for the first time. However, his plans take a drastic turn when he becomes embroiled in a police operation against a powerful drug cartel."Kaithi" was a critical and commercial success, praised for its innovative storytelling and fresh take on the action genre, and it solidified Lokesh Kanagaraj's reputation as a talented filmmaker in Tamil cinema. The film's success also led to discussions about a sequel, reflecting its significant impact on audiences.



2) Vikram

  • About:

    • "Vikram," released in 2022, is a Tamil action thriller directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring an ensemble cast led by Kamal Haasan, along with Vikram, Fahadh Faasil, and Vijay Sethupathi. The film follows the story of a fearless police officer, Vikram, who is tasked with uncovering a drug syndicate and confronting a dangerous antagonist, played by Vijay Sethupathi.The film’s success further established Lokesh Kanagaraj as a leading director and reaffirmed Kamal Haasan’s status as a legendary figure in Indian cinema.



3) Leo

  • About:

    • "Leo," released in 2023, is a Tamil action thriller directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, featuring Vijay in the lead role alongside a stellar cast that includes Trisha, Sanjay Dutt, and Arjun Sarja. The film follows the story of Leo, a mysterious and enigmatic character whose past comes back to haunt him, drawing him into a world of crime and conflict. It further cements his reputation as a master storyteller in contemporary Tamil cinema. The film was met with positive reviews and commercial success, resonating with audiences and fans of Vijay alike.
