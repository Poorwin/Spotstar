G V Prakash Kumar

G.V. Prakash Kumar is a prominent Indian music composer, playback singer, and actor, known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born on June 13, 1987, in Chennai, he is the nephew of legendary composer A.R. Rahman, which influenced his early interest in music. In addition to composing, G.V. has also lent his voice to many hit songs and has acted in several films, showcasing his multifaceted talentG.V. made his debut as a music composer at the young age of 17 with the film Veyyil, earning critical acclaim and establishing himself in the industry.
