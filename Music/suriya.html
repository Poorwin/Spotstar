Suriya

Suriya Sivakumar, commonly known as Suriya, is a prominent Tamil film actor and producer, born on July 23, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of veteran actor Sivakumar and has a younger brother, Karthi, who is also an actor. Suriya completed his schooling at St. Bede's Anglo Indian Higher Secondary School and later graduated with a Bachelorâ€™s degree in Commerce from the University of Chennai. He made his acting debut in 1997 with the film "Nerrukku Ner" and gained widespread recognition for his performances in films such as "Ghajini," "Singam," and "Soorarai Pottru." Suriya is known for his versatility and dedication to his roles, often undergoing significant transformations for his characters. He is also actively involved in social causes through his non-profit organization, Aasai Trust, focusing on education and health initiatives. Suriya is married to actress Jyothika, and the couple has two children. His contributions to cinema and society have earned him numerous awards and a loyal fanbase.
