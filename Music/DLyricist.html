Dhanush

Dhanush, born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja on July 28, 1983, in Chennai, is a celebrated Tamil actor, filmmaker, and playback singer. Coming from a film family, he is the son of director Kasthuri Raja and the younger brother of filmmaker Selvaraghavan. Dhanush made his acting debut in 2002 with Thulluvadho Ilamai and gained widespread recognition with Kadhal Kondein (2003). His versatile performances have garnered numerous awards, including a National Film Award for Best Actor for his role in Aadukalam (2011). Dhanush is also known for his musical talent, with the viral hit "Why This Kolaveri Di" showcasing his singing prowess. He married Aishwarya R. Dhanush, the daughter of legendary actor Rajinikanth, in 2004, and they have two sons. Beyond his film career, Dhanush is involved in philanthropic efforts, focusing on education and healthcare. He continues to push creative boundaries in both Tamil cinema and Hindi films, earning a reputation as one of the industry's most talented and multifaceted artists.


1) Amma Amma


  • Movie: Velailla Pattadhari
  • Theme: Mother's Love
  • About:

    • "Amma Amma" is a heartfelt song from the Tamil film VIP (Velai Illa Pattadhari), released in 2014. Composed by Anirudh Ravichander, this emotional track features poignant lyrics that express a son's deep love and gratitude for his mother. Sung by Anirudh himself and featuring powerful vocals, the song captures the essence of familial bonds and the sacrifices made by mothers.


2) Megham Karukkatha


  • Movie: Thiruchitrambalam
  • Theme: Romantic
  • About:

    • "Megham Karukkatha" is a soulful song from the Tamil film Thiruchitrambalam, released in 2022. Composed by Anirudh Ravichander, the song features the melodious voices of Arijit Singh and Shreya Ghoshal, creating a captivating duet that beautifully expresses emotions of love and longing.


3) Jodi Nilave


  • Movie: Thangamagan
  • Theme: Companionship
  • About:

    • Composed by Anirudh Ravichander and sung by the talented duo of Dhanush and Shreya Ghoshal, "Jodi Nilave" features a melodic and soothing tune that enchants listeners. The lyrics express heartfelt sentiments, celebrating the bond between the lead characters and their shared experiences.


4) Voda Voda Voda


  • Movie: Mayakkam Enna
  • Theme: Youthful exuberance
  • About:

    • Composed by GVP and sung by Dhanush and Aditi Rao Hydari, "Voda Voda" features an upbeat and catchy melody that perfectly complements its lively lyrics. The song is infused with a sense of energy and joy, inviting listeners to revel in the carefree spirit of youth.


5) Ilamai Thirumbudhey


  • Movie: Petta
  • Theme: Romantic
  • About:

    • Visually, "Ilamai Thirumbudhey" is accompanied by lively dance sequences featuring Rajinikanth, showcasing his iconic style and charisma.
