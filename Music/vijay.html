Thalapathy Vijay

Thalapathy Vijay, born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, comes from a family deeply rooted in the film industry. His father, S.A. Chandrasekhar, is a director and producer, while his mother, Shobha Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer.Thalapathy Vijay is a prominent Indian actor and playback singer known for his work in Tamil cinema. He has garnered a massive fan following due to his charismatic screen presence, dance skills, and versatile acting abilities. Vijay's films often blend action, drama, and romance, and many of them feature social themes.


1) Aaalaporan Tamizhan


  • Composer: A.R. Rahman
  • Lyricist: Vivek
  • Singers: Vijay, A.R. Rahman, and others
  • About:

    • The song celebrates Tamil culture, pride, and identity. It highlights the richness of Tamil language and heritage, emphasizing unity among Tamilians. The lyrics resonate with a sense of belonging and pride in being Tamil.


2)Naa Ready


  • Composer: Anirudh Ravichander
  • Lyricist: Vishnu Edavan
  • About:

    • "Naa Ready" is an upbeat and lively track that showcases Vijay's energetic performance and dance moves. The song's catchy beats and vibrant visuals make it a celebration of youth and self-assurance, appealing to fans and adding to the film's dynamic soundtrack.


3) Vaathi Coming


  • Composer: Anirudh Ravichander
  • Lyricist: Vivek

  • About:

The song showcases Vijay's charisma and dynamic dance moves, making it a favorite at parties and celebrations. The infectious rhythm and engaging visuals contribute to its widespread popularity.

With vibrant music and dynamic choreography, it has become a favorite at events and gatherings, reflecting the joy and excitement of love and life.


5) Sonna Puriyadhu
