Na.Muthukumar

Na Muthukumar was a renowned Tamil lyricist known for his poignant and heartfelt lyrics. Born on July 20, 1970, he made a significant impact in the Tamil music industry with his ability to blend deep emotions with everyday experiences. His work often explored themes of love, longing, and life's complexities, resonating with a wide audience.Tragically, he passed away in 2016, but his legacy continues to live on through his timeless lyrics that still touch hearts today. His contributions have left an indelible mark on Tamil cinema and music.


1) Oru Naalil


  • Movie: Pudhupettai
  • Theme: motivational
  • About:

    • "Oru Naalil" is a soulful and introspective song from the Tamil film "Pudhupettai," released in 2006. Composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja and sung by the talented Karthik, the song features poignant lyrics that reflect themes of hope, dreams, and the passage of time. The melody captures a sense of longing and contemplation, perfectly matching the film's gritty narrative.


2) Paravaiye engu irukkirai


  • Movie: Kattradhu Tamizh
  • Theme: Existential Introspection
  • About:

    • The theme of "Paravaiye Engu Irukkirai" revolves around existential introspection and the search for identity and purpose. The song explores feelings of alienation and longing, as the protagonist grapples with his place in the world. It reflects on life's uncertainties and the quest for meaning amidst chaos. The lyrics convey a deep emotional resonance, highlighting the struggles of self-discovery and the desire for connection, making it a poignant reflection on the human experience.
