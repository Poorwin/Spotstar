Sean Roldan

Sean Roldan is a talented Indian playback singer, music composer, and actor, known for his distinctive voice and contributions to Tamil cinema. With a background in music, he has carved out a niche for himself in the industry, gaining recognition for his versatile singing style that blends various genres. His dedication to his craft and his ability to convey emotion through music continue to inspire fans and fellow musicians alike, making him a prominent figure in the Tamil music scene.
