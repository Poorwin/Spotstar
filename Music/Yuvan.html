Yuvan Shankar Raja

Yuvan Shankar Raja is a renowned Indian music composer and playback singer, celebrated for his significant contributions to Tamil cinema. Born on August 31, 1979, he is the son of the legendary composer Ilaiyaraaja, and he has carved out his own unique identity in the industry.Yuvan Shankar Raja remains a prominent figure in the Tamil music scene, celebrated for his artistry and creativity.Beyond his work in film music, Yuvan is also recognized for his engaging performances and collaborations with various artists.
