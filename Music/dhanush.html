Dhanush

Dhanush, born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja on July 28, 1983, in Chennai, is a celebrated Tamil actor, filmmaker, and playback singer. Coming from a film family, he is the son of director Kasthuri Raja and the younger brother of filmmaker Selvaraghavan. Dhanush made his acting debut in 2002 with Thulluvadho Ilamai and gained widespread recognition with Kadhal Kondein (2003). His versatile performances have garnered numerous awards, including a National Film Award for Best Actor for his role in Aadukalam (2011). Dhanush is also known for his musical talent, with the viral hit "Why This Kolaveri Di" showcasing his singing prowess. He married Aishwarya R. Dhanush, the daughter of legendary actor Rajinikanth, in 2004, and they have two sons. Beyond his film career, Dhanush is involved in philanthropic efforts, focusing on education and healthcare. He continues to push creative boundaries in both Tamil cinema and Hindi films, earning a reputation as one of the industry's most talented and multifaceted artists.
