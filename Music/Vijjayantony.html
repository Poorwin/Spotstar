Vijay Antony

Vijay Antony is a multifaceted Indian musician, actor, and filmmaker known for his significant contributions to the Tamil film industry. He initially gained recognition as a music composer, crafting memorable soundtracks that resonate with audiences. His ability to blend traditional and contemporary styles has set him apart in the industry.Vijay Antony continues to make a significant impact in Tamil cinema, captivating audiences with his artistry and commitment.


1) Nooru Samigal


  • Movie: Pichaikkaran
  • Composer: Vijay Antony
  • Theme: Mother's Love
  • About:

    • "Nooru Samigal" from the movie Pichaikkaran is a deeply emotional song that beautifully captures the essence of a mother's love and sacrifice. Composed by the talented Vijay Antony, who also stars in the film, the song features poignant lyrics that reflect the profound bond between a mother and her child.
