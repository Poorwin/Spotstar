Aniruth Ravichandhar

Anirudh Ravichander is a talented Indian music composer and playback singer, widely recognized for his contributions to Tamil cinema. Born on October 16, 1990, in Chennai, he shot to fame with his debut soundtrack for the film 3, which featured the viral hit "Why This Kolaveri Di." This song not only made him a household name but also revolutionized the way music is marketed in Indian cinema. Beyond film music, he is also known for his independent projects and concerts, showcasing his versatility as an artist. Anirudh's youthful energy and creative vision continue to shape the contemporary music scene in Tamil cinema.
