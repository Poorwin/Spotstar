Vaali

Vaali, born as T. S. Narayanasamy on 29th April 1931, was a legendary Tamil lyricist and poet known for his profound contributions to Tamil cinema. With a career spanning over five decades, he penned thousands of songs, becoming one of the most celebrated lyricists in the industry.Vaali began his career in the 1950s and quickly gained recognition for his unique style and ability to capture complex emotions in his lyrics.Some of his most memorable works include songs from films like Ninaithale Inikkum, Baashha, and Sivaji, showcasing his versatility across various genres.


1) Kanmani Anbodu Kadhalan


  • Movie: Guna
  • Theme: Love
  • About:

    • "Kanmani Anbodu" is a romantic duet from the Tamil film "Guna," which was released in 1991. The song features a passionate interplay between the lead characters, showcasing their deep emotional connection. Sung by S. P. Balasubrahmanyam and K. S. Chithra, the melody is enchanting, blending beautifully with the evocative lyrics that express love and longing. The visuals depict scenic landscapes and intimate moments, enhancing the song's romantic essence. Overall, it's a heartfelt expression of love that remains memorable in Tamil cinema.


2) Mustafa Mustafa


  • Movie: Kadhal Desam
  • Theme: Friendship
  • About:

    • "Mustafa Mustafa" is a vibrant and energetic song from the Tamil film "Kadhal Desam," released in 1996. Sung by A. R. Rahman and S. P. Balasubrahmanyam, this track features catchy rhythms and playful lyrics that capture the carefree spirit of youth and love. The song is set against colorful backdrops, showcasing lively dance sequences and the chemistry between the lead characters. It combines elements of romance and fun, making it a memorable anthem of friendship and affection. The infectious energy and catchy tune have made it a favorite among fans of Tamil cinema.


3) Inji Iduppazhaga


  • Movie: Devar Magan
  • Theme: Romantic
  • About:

    • "Inji Idupazhaga" is a classic song from the Tamil film "Devar Magan," released in 1992. Sung by the legendary S. Janaki, this enchanting melody beautifully blends romance and lyrical depth. The song features evocative lyrics that celebrate the beauty and charm of a beloved. Visually, it is set in a picturesque landscape, showcasing the lead characters' chemistry and their tender moments together. The soulful rendition, along with its rich orchestration, makes "Inji Idupazhaga" a timeless favorite, capturing the essence of love and admiration in Tamil cinema.


4) Enna Vilai Azhagae


  • Movie: Kadhalar Dhinam
  • Theme: Love
  • About:

    • "Enna Vilai Azhage" is a captivating romantic song from the Tamil film "Kadhalar Dhinam," released in 1999. Sung by the melodious Unni Menon and composed by A. R. Rahman, the song features beautiful, poetic lyrics that express deep admiration and longing for a loved one. The visuals complement the music with stunning locations and heartfelt interactions between the lead pair, emphasizing their emotional connection. With its enchanting melody and expressive vocals, "Enna Vilai Azhage" remains a beloved classic, showcasing the essence of love and romance in Tamil cinema.


5) Athiradee


  • Movie: Sivaji
  • Theme: Classic
  • About:

    • "Adiradi" is a high-energy song from the Tamil film "Sivaji," released in 2007. Composed by A. R. Rahman and featuring energetic vocals by A. R. Rahman himself along with others, this track blends peppy rhythms with vibrant beats, making it a celebration of joy and exuberance.
