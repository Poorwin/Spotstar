Ajithkumar

Ajith Kumar, widely known as Thala, is a prominent Tamil film actor born on May 1, 1971, in Hyderabad, India. He is the son of P. Subramaniam and Mohini, and he has a sister named Anupama. Ajith pursued his education in Chennai and initially aspired to become a race car driver, a passion he later pursued alongside his acting career.Ajith made his acting debut in 1990 with the film "Prema Pusthakam" and gained recognition with his performances in films like "Vaali," "Kandukondain Kandukondain," and "Mankatha." Known for his versatility and charisma, he has garnered a massive fan following over the years.
