Vijay Sethupathi

Vijay Sethupathi is a renowned Indian actor, producer, and screenwriter, celebrated for his impactful performances in Tamil cinema. Born on January 16, 1978, in Rajapalayam, Tamil Nadu, he completed his education at Dhanraj Baid Jain College in Chennai.Vijay is married to Jessie Sethupathi, and they have two children—a son and a daughter. He made his acting debut in the critically acclaimed film "Thenmerku Paruvakatru" in 2010 and quickly rose to fame for his versatile roles. Some of his notable films include "Naduvula Konjam Pakkatha Kanni," "Sethupathi," "Vikram Vedha," and "Super Deluxe."
